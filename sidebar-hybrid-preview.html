This is a preview of the hybrid sidebar design for NexLet.

What changed from current:

  • 3 primary groups (Portfolio, Staying Legal, Money) — always expanded
  • 1 collapsed "Tools & Records" — contains 6 low-frequency items (Templates, Docs, Contractors, Insurance, Inspections, Inventory Reports)
  • Dashboard moved into Portfolio group — not floating orphan at top
  • Calendar moved into Staying Legal — cert expiry dates, inspection scheduling, maintenance deadlines all live together
  • Maintenance + Compliance merged under "Staying Legal" — Awaab's Law makes maintenance a compliance concern
  • AI Assistant extracted from nav items — now a standalone card below groups (always visible, not buried)
  • Feedback moved to footer — tiny link, not a nav item with an icon
  • Badge counts on group headers — see "Staying Legal ⚠ 3" at a glance without expanding
  • Tools group collapsed by default — 6 items hidden until needed
  • 15 items → 9 visible, 6 collapsed — cleaner first impression
